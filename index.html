About Me

Hello! My name is Nataly, and I am a passionate front-end web developer and occupational therapist. I have a deep love for creating visually appealing, user-friendly websites that provide an excellent user experience. My journey into web development started from a desire to merge creativity with technology, leading me to become proficient in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript.

In addition to my technical skills, I bring a unique perspective to web development from my background as an occupational therapist. This experience has equipped me with a keen understanding of user needs, accessibility, and the importance of creating intuitive interfaces that can be easily navigated by all users, including those with disabilities.

My dual career paths allow me to approach problems with both a technical and a human-centered perspective. Whether I am designing a website or helping a client improve their quality of life, I am committed to making a positive impact through my work.
